# Play Store Feature Graphic Ideas (1024×500 px)

Use these as briefs for Canva, Figma, or another design tool.

---

## Idea 1: Minimal & Clean
- **Background:** Soft teal gradient (#0D9488 → #14B8A6)
- **Left:** Large toilet emoji or simple icon
- **Center:** Bold white "Ploop"
- **Right:** Tagline: "Find clean toilets nearby"
- **Style:** Minimal, lots of white space

---

## Idea 2: Map-Focused
- **Background:** Light gray (#F3F4F6)
- **Left:** Stylized map with pins
- **Right:** "Ploop" in bold, "Discover & review nearby toilets"
- **Accent:** Teal or blue for icons and text

---

## Idea 3: Trust & Ratings
- **Background:** White with subtle pattern
- **Center:** "Ploop" + "Rated by real people"
- **Below:** Row of 5 stars and "Reviews • Maps • Filters"
- **Accent:** Teal or green for trust

---

## Idea 4: Action-Oriented
- **Background:** Gradient (teal → blue)
- **Left:** Phone mockup with map
- **Right:** "Find. Rate. Go." in bold
- **Style:** Modern, app-store style

---

## Idea 5: Playful
- **Background:** Soft yellow (#FEF3C7)
- **Center:** "Ploop" with toilet emoji
- **Tagline:** "Your bathroom finder"
- **Style:** Light, friendly, not too serious

---

## Requirements
- **Size:** 1024 × 500 px
- **Format:** PNG or JPEG, max 15 MB
- **App icon:** 512 × 512 px (use `mobile/assets/icon.png`)
