Ploop Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

Ploop ("we", "our", or "the app") helps you find and review nearby toilets. This policy describes what data we collect and how we use it.

Data We Collect

Location. We use your device's location to show nearby toilets and provide directions. Location is used only while the app is in use and is not stored beyond what is needed for your session.

Account information. If you sign in with Google, Apple, or email, we store your email and display name to associate your reviews, favorites, and reports with your account.

Photos and camera. If you upload photos of toilets, we may access your camera or photo library. Photos are used only for the toilet listings you contribute and are stored on our servers.

Reviews and content. When you submit reviews, reports, photos, or suggestions, we store this content to provide the service and improve toilet information for all users.

Device identifier. We may use a device identifier (not tied to your identity) for analytics and to prevent abuse.

Push notifications. If you are an admin, we may send push notifications for user suggestions. You can disable notifications in your device settings.

How We Use Data

We use your data to:

  • Show nearby toilets and provide navigation
  • Display and manage your reviews, favorites, and lists
  • Improve the app and understand usage patterns
  • Respond to support requests

Third-Party Services

We use:

  • Google – Sign-in, maps, and directions (subject to Google's Privacy Policy)
  • Apple – Sign-in (subject to Apple's Privacy Policy)
  • Expo – App infrastructure and push notifications
  • Gaode Maps – Map display in certain regions

Data Retention

We retain your account data and content for as long as your account exists. You can delete your account or request deletion of your data by contacting us.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your data. Contact us to exercise these rights.
